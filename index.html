Industry Skills

Created as a joint initiative between NUS iDP and Invigilo, Invictus AI Club aims to bring students who are passionate about cutting edge AI and IoT technology together.

Students are given the opportunity to develop systems for real world clients and applications. Invictus seeks to ground AI and IoT education in realistic settings through projects set in real-world contexts.

Real World Problems

Invigilo provides relevant training materials, workshops, and sample projects for students to learn about AI and its implementation in the real world.

Additionally, they have strong connections in the start-up and industrial ecosystems, allowing them to find clients who have needs for AI-based solutions to market and deploy studentsâ€™ projects.

Work Accredidation

Projects that are implemented in the market will be credited to the students that worked on it.

Why Join Us?

Watch the video to learn more about Invgilo

Invictus offers students the opportunity to work on a year-long, real-world AI-based project that may eventually be deployed in industry. These projects will be in the form of a problem statement that will require the full development of a deployable prototype from the students, with guidance and advice from Invigilo wherever needed. Projects offer the opportunity to develop a product with a real client and problem statement, requiring them to meet deadlines and create an entire solution for a real-world application. Students will also receive recognition for embarking on such a project.

Invictus is open to everyone, regardless of faculty and experience.
